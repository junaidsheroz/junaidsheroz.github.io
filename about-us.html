A Brief History
About UVA India.

We have established the United Volunteers Association (UVA) under the guidelines and regulations of Section 8 Company registered under the Companies Act of 2013.. United Volunteers Association (UVA) is an India-based non-profit charitable organization that holds approval under section 12A & 80G of the Income Tax Act. It is our collective commitment to fulfill our responsibilities towards humanity, irrespective of considerations like race, gender, ethnicity, class, location, religion, color, cultural diversity, and social background, with a special focus on addressing the needs of the population living below the poverty line.

Coordinate & Promote

To conduct, coordinate and promote various educational, social, cultural, healthcare, and economic upliftment activities to benefit underprivileged people of economic and socially backward societies.

Solicit Funds

To raise and solicit funds from the donors and grants from corporate companies to carry out the welfare programs directly or in cooperation with other non-profit charitable organizations working to eradicate poverty, improve quality of life and to promote economic development by implementing educational and healthcare programs, especially for underprivileged people, in the economically backward areas.

Raise Funds

To raise, solicit, receive charitable funds and donations to carry out worthy humanitarian programs and rapid relief operations during natural causes like famine, Earthquake, flood, fire , ethnic clashes and other calamities of similar nature either directly or in cooperation with other non-profit organizations.

Provide Assistance

To provide charitable assistance to empower the underprivileged people of the society through socio-economic development programs such as micro-finance for setting up small businesses, self-employment schemes, food packets distribution, warm clothes distribution during winter etc.

Modern Education

To provide modern education and training to the economically backward students in the society.

Provide Training

To provide education and training to the poor without discrimination of caste and creed by enabling them to earn livelihood after this training

Scholarships

To provide aid, financial assistance, and scholarships to the brilliant, poor, and deserving students for their education.

Financial Assistance

To provide financial assistance to elderly people, widows, orphans, poor girls marriages and to render assistance for relief of the poor and destitute

Medical Aid

To aid for medical treatment, medicines and other medical facilities to serve the poor and deserving patients.

UVA's Mission

To provide charitable assistance to implement educational development, social welfare, charity, hunger, cultural, healthcare and emergency relief programs in economically backward areas with a focus on projects that promote self-employment to ensure sustainable individual and community growth.

Our Vision

UVA envisions a harmonious and inclusive India, where every individual, regardless of their circumstances, enjoys equal access to opportunities, basic necessities, and the full spectrum of human rights. Our vision is to create a society where poverty, inequality, and social disparities are mere remnants of the past, and where compassion, empowerment, and sustainable development are the pillars of our nation. We aspire to be the catalyst for positive change, working tirelessly to uplift the marginalized and create a better, more equitable future for all
